Adventures

Nainital is a picturesque hill station in Uttarakhand, nestled in the Kumaon foothills of the outer Himalayas. With its rich history, scenic beauty, and abundant trekking opportunities, it is the perfect destination for adventurers and nature lovers.

Guided Trekking & Homestays

  • Home Stay Experience (Overnight in Pali-Pangot, 2100m)
    Rs. 20,500 / 2 persons
    Experience local hospitality with an Indian family, savor home-cooked meals, and enjoy waterfalls and sunset views.
  • Pangot Trek (2100m)
    Rs. 21,500 / 2 persons
    A 24km round-trip trek through Naini Peak and Kilbury with birdwatching and breathtaking Himalayan views.
  • Pangot & Haryal Treks (2 Nights, 3 Days)
    Rs. 27,500 / 2 persons
    Explore Ghanaial Dev Temple, wildlife, and village life over a scenic 32km round-trip trek.

Scenic Viewpoints & Nature Walks

  • Naini Peak (2611m): A 6km hike to the highest point in Nainital with panoramic views of Nanda Devi and the Himalayas.
  • Snow View (2270m): A 2.5km trail leading to stunning Himalayan vistas.
  • Camel's Back (2600m): A serene 5.5km walk through dense forest.
  • Khurpa Tal: A picturesque lake, ideal for fishing and swimming.

Local Day Treks (Rs. 3,500/day for 4 persons)

  • Nainital → Snow View → Naini Peak → Camel's Back → Land’s End → Tiffin Top (16km round trip)
  • Nainital → Kilbury → Pangot → Naini Peak (19km round trip)
  • Nainital → Kahala Garden → Lal Pani Waterfall (14km round trip)

Trekking to Local Villages

Experience authentic Kumaoni village life, taste traditional dishes, and enjoy sunrise views over the Himalayas.

  • Ghugukhan Trek (28km round trip)
    Includes basic Indian-style accommodation, all meals, and a cultural experience with a local family.

Bird Watching (Rs. 4,000/day for 4 persons)

Uttarakhand is home to nearly half of India's bird species, making it a paradise for birdwatchers.

Serious Trekking Expeditions

For seasoned trekkers, we arrange high-altitude Himalayan treks, including:

  • Pindari Glacier (3820m)
  • Milam Glacier (4267m)
  • Roop Kund (5029m)
  • Valley of Flowers (3650m)
  • Char Dham Pilgrimage (Badrinath, Kedarnath, Yamunotri, Gangotri)
